🐹 Why Wildlife Guardian?

The European Hamster is a beautiful wild species, but it is now Critically Endangered. Modern farming and growing cities have broken up its habitat, leaving it with nowhere to live safely.

This app was created to raise awareness. By taking care of your virtual hamster and completing healthy habits, you learn how specialized and vulnerable these creatures are in the wild.
